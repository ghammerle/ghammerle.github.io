Welcome to California

California is the 3rd largest state of the United States by land mass and largest state by population. California is on the west coast of the United States. California borders 3 state Arizona, Nevada and Oregon.
According to Wikipedia, California became the 31st state in 1850 after President Millard Fillmore signed the California Statehood act. Coincidentally this law was passed by the 31st Congress of the United States("California Statehood Act," n.d.).
